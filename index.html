This website was created as a project for my HTML course on Coursera. The goal of the assignment is to practice building and structuring a webpage using fundamental HTML elements.

Content of the Website

The site features a collection of music artists and songs that I enjoy. Each entry includes links that allow visitors to explore and listen to the featured tracks on external platforms.

MY CURRENT FAVORITE ARTISTS

  1. BOY IN SPACE

    Boy in Space is the stage name of Swedish singer-songwriter Robin Lundbäck. He was born on 17 May 1994 in Alingsås, Sweden and makes pop and R&B music. He first gained attention as a member of the band JTR before starting his solo career in 2018. As Boy in Space, he became known for emotional pop songs like “Cold,” “Drown,” and “Caroline”. His music often focuses on themes such as love, heartbreak, and growing up.

    Some of my favorite songs from BOY IN SPACE:

    1. Caroline
    2. Picking Flowers
    3. Who's Crying When I'm Leaving
    4. Too Much To Lose
    5. 7Up

  2. SAID THE SKY

    Trevor Christensen, known professionally as Said the Sky, is an American DJ, music producer, and electronic musician from Denver, Colorado. He was born on 12 May 1993 and is known for creating melodic EDM and future bass music. He gained recognition through collaborations with artists like Illenium and became popular for emotional electronic tracks such as “Rush Over Me”.

    Some of my favorite songs from SAID THE SKY:

    1. In The End
    2. Spider
    3. Emotion Sickness
    4. Stolen
    5. Potions

  3. LUCAS RICCIOTTI

    Lucas Ricciotti is a composer, producer, and songwriter specializing in epic and cinematic music for video games and visual media. Born(2001) in Rosario, Argentina and currently living in Santiago, Chile, Lucas began playing piano at the age of 13, where he discovered a deep interest in music from video games and films. He started composing instrumental music at 15, exploring different genres and releasing his first album in 2019. Ricciotti has worked on the music of titles such as Whiskerwood, Frontiers Reach, and StarFlight. He is also a follower of Jesus and seeks to glorify God and spread the gospel through his music, creating Christian albums and songs to make Jesus known.

    Some of my favorite musics from LUCAS RICCIOTTI:

    1. Lonely Fox
    2. Honor
    3. Exploring New Lands
    4. Facing The Adversity
    5. The Lord Of Armies

  4. ARIJIT SINGH

    Arijit Singh is an Indian singer, music producer, and composer from Jiaganj, West Bengal, India. Born on 25 April 1987, he is widely known as one of the most influential voices in Hindi and Bengali film music. He first gained attention after participating in the reality show Fame Gurukul in 2005, but rose to major fame with the song “Tum Hi Ho” from the 2013 film Aashiqui 2, which earned him a Filmfare Award for Best Male Playback Singer. Arijit Singh has won multiple awards, including two National Film Awards, several Filmfare Awards, and the Padma Shri in 2025. Known for his emotional singing style and versatility across genres, he has become one of the most popular and streamed Indian artists worldwide.

    Some of my favorite musics from ARIJIT SINGH:

    1. Agar Tum Sath Ho
    2. Samjhawan
    3. Apna Bana Le
    4. Love Me Thoda Aur
    5. Sooraj Dooba Hain

  5. POST MALONE

    Post Malone (born Austin Richard Post) is an American singer, rapper, and songwriter from Syracuse, New York, United States. Born on 4 July 1995, he is known for blending genres such as hip-hop, pop, R&B, rock, and country in his music. He gained major recognition in 2015 with his debut single “White Iverson”, which went viral online and led to a recording contract with Republic Records. The success of the song helped launch his career and led to the release of his debut album Stoney in 2016. Post Malone later became one of the most popular artists of the late 2010s, releasing hit songs such as “Rockstar,” “Sunflower,” “Circles,” and “Congratulations”. His music is widely known for its genre-blending style and strong commercial success worldwide.

    Some of my favorite musics from POST MALONE:

    1. Sunflower
    2. Losers
    3. I Had Some Help
    4. Better Now
    5. Pour Me A Drink
